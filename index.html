I was constantly checking my phone — again and again — waiting for one notification. And I came running, because I hoped that message was from you.

I love you. And I'm sorry for every misunderstanding that grew between us — all the times silence did the damage words never could.

I should have called more. I should have tried harder. I should have made you feel as certain of me as I was certain of you.

You told me what you'd carried — all that you'd let go of — and still chose to stay. Thank you. Thank you for that grace.

I'm sorry you had to go through any of it. Maybe I wasn't the person you needed then. But I am trying — every single day — to become someone who gives you a truly happy life ahead.
